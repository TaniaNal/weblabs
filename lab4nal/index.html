The History Fitness

As we enter the 21st century, one of the greatest accomplishments to be celebrated is the continuous pursuit of fitness since the beginning
of man’s existence. Throughout prehistoric time, man's quest for fitness has been driven by a desire to survive through hunting
and gathering. Today, though no longer driven by subsistence requirements, fitness remains paramount to health and well-being. This article will highlight
historical events and influential individuals who have shaped the history of fitness beginning with primitive man up to the foundation of the modern fitness movement.
